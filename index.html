Our Services/Products

We have a wide range of services and products that span across all socio-economic groups

Electric Tricycle

This is the first to be manufactured in our range of products. It has the capacity to go up to a range of 200km on one charge, with a battery warranty of five years and have over 5000 battery live cycle. It takes 30minutes to charge it to full capacity.

City Electric Bus

The seven seater City Electric bus is the second in our range of electric vehicles with a capacity to go 150km on one charge at a speed of 110KMPH. The electric motor power is 15KW, the battery is 10Kwhr lithium ion batteries, it has a minimum life span of 5000 cycles, charge time of 35minutes and a front wheel drive system.

Phoenix Future

Phoenix Future is our very own baby, the first electric bus to ever be manufactured in Africa from scratch to finish with over 70% materials locally sourced. It has all the modern facilities that a 2021/2022 model of any vehicle should have with voice command systems in local languages. It goes up to 200KM on one charge, with 20Kwhr lithium ion batteries, 15KW electric motor power, minimum of 5000 battery life cycle and gets fully charged in 35minutes.

Battery Storage

With the national grid failing over the years, places such communities, estates, factories, offices, hospitals etc., who need stable power supply for their daily operations have to explore an alternative and reliable sources of power. Renewable energy has been proven to be the only affordable and sustainable means of generating electricity.

Ceo & Founder
